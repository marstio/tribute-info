Seán William McLoughlin

Irish YouTuber Who Goes Beyond Gaming

Seán McLoughlin on the red carpet at "The Game Awards"

  • Seán William McLoughlin, publicly known as Jacksepticeye, is an Irish YouTuber who began his journey by uploading gaming videos to YouTube in December 2012.
  • His content mainly features Let's Plays, comedy videos, vlogs, and reaction videos. He famously started his videos with a high five and the catchphrase "Top of the morning to ya, laddies!"
  • This iconic catchphrase even led to the creation of his coffee brand Top of the Mornin' Coffee.
  • He often organizes charity livestreams, notably the annual Thankmas event, raising over $25 million for charities like BLM, Red Nose Day, New Story, and World Central Kitchen.
